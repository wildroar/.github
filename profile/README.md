# Wild Roar

**A two-person digital agency for travel and outdoor brands.**

We design and build websites, web apps, and brand systems for companies that help people enjoy their vacations — rental managers, tour operators, outfitters, lodges, and the companies that support them. Based in Paonia, Colorado.

[wildroar.co](https://wildroar.co)

---

## What we do

- **Web development** — custom web apps, marketing sites, integrations, and the boring-but-critical infrastructure behind them.
- **UX & UI design** — research, information architecture, interaction design, and interfaces that put the content first.
- **Branding** — identity and visual systems for brands that need more than a logo.

## What we work with

**Frontend**
React · TypeScript · Next.js · Astro · Vite · Tailwind · GSAP · Framer Motion

**WordPress**
Custom themes and plugins · Twig templating · Bedrock · Composer · Migrations and automation

**Platforms & infrastructure**
Cloudflare · DigitalOcean · WP Engine · Supabase · Vercel • Custom hosting architecture · performance tuning

## Working with us

We take on a small number of projects at a time and stay involved through launch and beyond. If you run a travel or outdoor business and need a partner for a site redesign, a new tool, or ongoing technical work, get in touch at [hello@wildroar.co](mailto:hello@wildroar.co).
